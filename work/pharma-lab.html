Healthcare Data Visualization B2B

Pharma Lab

Clinical trial data was sitting in spreadsheets. The insights were there — they just needed a way to surface.

Context

A biotech startup running Phase II clinical trials needed to replace a manual reporting workflow with a real-time analytics dashboard. Researchers were spending 12+ hours per week consolidating data from disparate sources into Excel reports. Regulatory deadlines were being missed not because the data wasn't there, but because no one could surface it fast enough.

The team had four separate data systems: lab results, patient logs, dosage records, and adverse event reports. None of them talked to each other. The "dashboard" was a shared Google Sheet that broke every time someone added a new trial cohort.

We were brought in to design and build the data layer and visualization system from scratch — from information architecture through to a production-ready interface.

Our Solution

We designed a unified dashboard that pulled from all four data sources into a single interface, with key metrics surfacing automatically based on trial phase and protocol. Custom alert thresholds replaced manual checking. Exportable regulatory-ready PDF summaries replaced the weekly Excel ritual — a summary that previously took three hours now generated in under 30 seconds.
