Gamification HR Tech Product Design

Nawaiam

Traditional assessments measure what candidates know. We redesigned the experience to reveal who they really are.

Context

Nawaiam is a gamified assessment platform used by companies across Latin America to identify behavioral profiles during recruitment. The client had strong IP — a validated psychometric model — but the experience of taking and interpreting the assessment was fragmented, slow, and unintuitive. Completion rates were below 60%.

Their B2B clients — HR teams at mid-to-large enterprises — struggled to interpret results and export them into existing ATS workflows. The dashboard was data-rich but decision-poor: everything was visible, nothing was actionable.

Our engagement covered both sides of the product: the candidate-facing game experience and the HR results dashboard. Two audiences, one coherent system.

Our Solution

We rebuilt the candidate experience around flow and momentum — removing friction points, reordering screens to create a sense of progress, and redesigning the scoring visualization so results could be read at a glance. For the HR dashboard, we introduced role-based views, one-click PDF exports, and a search and filter layer that mapped directly to industry-standard competency frameworks, turning raw psychometric data into hiring decisions.
