Nawaiam Dashboard

Designing a gamified emotional intelligence assessment that turns behavioral data into actionable insights for organizations.

Context

Nawaiam was building a different kind of assessment tool. Instead of traditional questionnaires, the goal was to evaluate emotional intelligence and behavioral traits through an interactive experience. The challenge was not only technological — it was conceptual.

How do you transform psychological frameworks into a digital product that feels engaging, credible, and measurable at the same time? The platform needed to speak to two very different audiences:

  • • Candidates, who should feel immersed rather than evaluated.
  • • Organizations, who required structured, reliable data to support decision-making.

At the same time, the product had to position itself in a competitive HR-tech landscape, where credibility and clarity are critical.

Our Solution

We worked at the intersection of product strategy, UX design, and behavioral modeling. First, we translated psychological models into a playable narrative structure. The experience was designed as an interactive journey where each decision generates behavioral signals.

We structured the product around three key principles:

  • • Immersive interaction – The assessment feels like a game, not a test.
  • • Data integrity – Every interaction maps to measurable behavioral variables.
  • • Clarity for organizations – Results are presented through structured dashboards designed for HR teams and decision-makers.

From information architecture to interaction flows, we ensured that gameplay and data collection were aligned. The result is a platform that balances engagement with analytical rigor — transforming soft skills into structured insights.

Outcomes
  • • Gamified assessment replacing traditional forms
  • • Structured behavioral data model
  • • Dual interface: candidate experience + HR dashboard
  • • Clear positioning within the HR-tech ecosystem
